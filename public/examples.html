How It Works

1

Embeddings

Each word is mapped to a vector capturing semantic meaning. Similar words have similar vectors.

2

Rhyming

Phonetic patterns identify which words rhyme, creating additional connections.

3

Layout

A force-directed algorithm positions words in 2D space, clustering related concepts.

4

Generation

Poems are created by walking through the graph, connecting semantically related words.
